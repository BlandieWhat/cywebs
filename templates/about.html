WELCOME TO CHENNAI YOUNGSTERS



Chennai Youngsters is a Non Profitable Organisation Compiled with Enthusiastic Youngsters in and around Chennai with the common objectives of working for bringing in sustainable development by taking step for eradicating poverty, Providing elementary educational, Awareness among the masses, Environment sustainability. We aim at Empowering and organising Youth to achieve the vision of success and transformation of modern society we live in and make them to be a responsible citizen of India.

Goals and Motives

Our on going goal is to bridge the inequalities in society by
improving the standards of education and encouraging the youth to volunteer their time for the nation’s progress.
We have reached a Good Peak by direct and indirect involvement of our volunteers and Executive committee..

Different Platforms

With volunteers from various fields we reach our destination so quickly and easily.

Emerging Projects

The volunteers are allowed to pitch in their ideas to the executive committee and they get to host and lead .

Fulfilling Request

The requests like blood donation, educational payments are completed immediately in few hours after the request have been passed.

Environmental Nourishment

Our team works on environment development and educate people about the environment we living.

Social Development

Our members also explore and reach the society by RTI development, Situational Projects, Self Growth Programmes etc.

Arising Youths

Stepping stone for your Self-Growth, Networking with people in various fields, learn teamwork, leadership, Communication, Public Speaking etc. are some of the perks of being a member of Chennai Youngsters.

Every projects processed in Chennai Youngsters comes under the three initatives we launched.

  • KARPIPOM - கற்பிப்போம்.

    KARPIPOM deals with helping students in creating a bright future for themselves and mentoring them in their interested career choices. It also deals with various academic motivation to the upcoming students.

  • VIDHAIPOM - விதைப்போம்.

    VIDHAIPOM deals with the environmental changes and care that is needed to make our surroundings a better place to be in. Helping the nature to be loved and lived.

  • KAAPOM - காப்போம்.

    KAAPOM deals with the rehabilitation of abandoned people and helping senior members of our society with their medical and basic essentials.
